Information We Collect

⚠️ CRITICAL DISCLOSURE - VPN SERVICE

THIS APP USES VPN TECHNOLOGY TO DETECT AND SAVE MEDIA CONTENT

SaveIt uses Android VPN Service (VpnService) to detect videos and photos from other applications. This is essential for the app's core functionality.

What This Means:

  • Local VPN Only: The VPN runs entirely on your device - we do NOT route your traffic through external servers
  • Media Detection: We analyze network traffic to identify video and photo URLs
  • No Data Upload: Detected media information stays on your device
  • Privacy Protected: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your browsing history, passwords, or personal data

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • ❌ Your browsing history
  • ❌ Passwords or login credentials
  • ❌ Personal messages or communications
  • ❌ Banking or payment information
  • ❌ Location data

You cannot opt-out of VPN usage as it is essential for the core functionality. If you do not agree, please do not install or use this app.
