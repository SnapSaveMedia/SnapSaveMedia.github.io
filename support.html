Support & FAQ

How do I use SaveIt?

1. Open SaveIt and tap "Start Saving"
2. Open any app (Instagram, TikTok, etc.)
3. Browse videos or photos
4. Return to SaveIt to see detected media
5. Tap to preview and save

Which apps are supported?

SaveIt works with most popular apps including Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter, and many more. If you find an app that doesn't work, please let us know!

Why do I need to enable VPN?

SaveIt uses VPN technology to detect media content. This is a local VPN that only works on your device - we don't route your traffic through external servers. Your privacy is protected.

Is my data safe?

Yes! We don't collect or store your saved videos or photos. All content stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I save copyrighted content?

SaveIt is for personal use only. You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to save and use any content. Please respect copyright laws and terms of service of other platforms.

What's the difference between Free and VIP?

Free Version: Unlimited saves with ads
VIP Version: No ads, unlimited history, HD priority, batch download

How do I cancel my VIP subscription?

1. Open Google Play Store
2. Tap Menu > Subscriptions
3. Find SaveIt and tap Cancel

The app is not detecting videos. What should I do?

Try these steps:
1. Make sure VPN is enabled in SaveIt
2. Restart the app
3. Check if you granted all required permissions
4. Try clearing the app cache
5. Reinstall the app if the problem persists

Where are my saved files?

Saved files are stored in your device's Gallery/Photos app, in the "SaveIt" folder. You can also access them from the SaveIt app's history.

Can I use SaveIt on multiple devices?

Yes! If you purchase VIP, it's linked to your Google account and works on all your Android devices.
